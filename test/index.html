Trevince Chalet

Treat yourself to a proper break with a boutique chalet experience in an idyllic Cornish Cove. Situated in the heart of 'Poldark Country', Trevaunance Cove is an ideal base from which to explore the sandy beaches and attractions of the Duchy of Cornwall, although many guests enter a state of deep relaxation and find it hard to tear themselves away from this picturesque Cove and St Agnes village with all the local opportunities for recreation and culture, yes, culture! Cosy getaway just 100 metres from the beach with lovely views. The Cornish coast path runs behind the chalet.

The Cove

You'll find the Cove at the end of a wooded valley half a mile from the village centre. Enjoy the 17th century pub, fish & chip shop, tea rooms and craft studios. A sheltered beach, the turquoise waters are a haven for bathers, surfers, seals, sea birds, dolphins and basking shark. It is a Site of Special Scientific Interest, Dark sky Area and Area of outstanding natural beauty. On your doorstep you'll find:

  • Surfing lessons and kayak tours
  • RNLI lifeboat station
  • Fishing catches landed on the slipway
  • Beach bar & restaurant
  • Beach café
  • Rockpools and ruined harbour
  • Cornish coast path
  • St. Agnes

St. Agnes

A proper village with a happy community and all the facilities you'll need. St Agnes was rated as one of the 3 best places to live and holiday (Sunday Times March 2017).

  • Local produce in the shops
  • Cafés, pubs, restaurants
  • Galleries, craft studios
  • Churches, museum, library
  • Live music and events
  • Superfast broadband
